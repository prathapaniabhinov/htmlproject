System Overview

This web-based placement interaction system enables companies to post job openings, students to apply for jobs, and placement staff to track and report placement records.

Role-based views are provided for Admin, Student, Employer, and Placement Officer to manage their respective tasks efficiently.

Active role: Student

Job Opportunities

Employers can post job listings, and students can browse and apply based on eligibility and interest.

Job Title

Application Status & Reports

Students track their application status, while placement officers and admins view overall placement statistics and reports.

User Roles and Responsibilities

Admin

System Management
  • Manage system settings and configurations.
  • Create and assign user roles and accounts.
  • Maintain placement data and backups.

Student

Job Seeker
  • Explore and search job opportunities.
  • Apply for suitable job positions.
  • Track application and offer status.

Employer

Recruiter
  • Post and manage job listings.
  • Review student applications and resumes.
  • Interact with shortlisted candidates.

Placement Officer

Coordinator
  • Monitor placement drives and records.
  • Generate placement statistics and reports.
  • Facilitate communication between employers and students.
